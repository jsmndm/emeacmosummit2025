🌟 Fun Facts About Dubai

  • Dubai is home to the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
  • The Palm Jumeirah is a manmade island visible from space.
  • Dubai Mall is the world’s largest shopping mall, featuring over 1,200 shops and an indoor aquarium.
  • Over 200 nationalities live in Dubai, making it one of the most culturally diverse cities globally.
  • Dubai enjoys over 300 days of sunshine each year.
  • Dubai Frame offers panoramic views of old and new Dubai.
  • Dubai Creek is the cultural heart of the city, with traditional abras and flamingos.
  • Dubai’s hospitality is world-famous, with luxury hotels and unique experiences.

Source: Visit Dubai(https://www.visitdubai.com/en/plan-your-trip)

🗺️ Dubai Travel Guide

  • Burj Khalifa: Tallest building, observation deck, open daily.
  • Dubai Mall: Shopping, aquarium, ice rink, open 10 AM–12 AM.
  • Dubai Frame: Iconic monument, panoramic views.
  • Dubai Creek: Heritage area, traditional markets, boat rides.
  • Museum of the Future: Futuristic exhibits, open daily.
  • Palm Jumeirah: Beaches, restaurants, The View at The Palm.
  • Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood: Traditional architecture, souks.
  • Dubai Miracle Garden: Largest flower garden, seasonal hours.

Getting Around: Metro, taxis, ride-sharing apps.
Currency: AED (Dirham), US$1 = AED3.67.
Emergency: Police 999, Ambulance 998, Fire 997.
Power: 220/240 volts, UK-style plugs.
Free SIM card for tourists at the airport.

Source: Visit Dubai(https://www.visitdubai.com/en/plan-your-trip)
